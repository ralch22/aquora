# About Aquora

## Engineered in Dubai, built for the Gulf

Aquora began with a simple frustration shared by engineers and villa owners alike: too much of the equipment sold into the region's pools and water features was specified on price rather than performance, then left without proper support once the installer had driven away. We started in Dubai to do the opposite — to bring genuinely engineered water systems to the GCC, backed by people who stand behind them.

From a single workshop and showroom, Aquora has grown into a trusted supplier of pool, spa, pond and fountain equipment for residential and commercial projects across the Emirates. Our approach has never changed. We treat every pool as a piece of engineering — a balance of hydraulics, filtration, chemistry and energy — and we specify accordingly. That discipline is what separates water that simply looks clean from water that is genuinely safe, stable and efficient to run in the Gulf climate.

## A complete catalogue, engineered to work together

Our house product lines cover the full water cycle. We supply variable-speed circulation pumps, high-flow sand and cartridge filtration, energy-efficient heat pumps and heating, automated dosing and sanitisation, and precise LED lighting and control systems. Beyond the swimming pool, our range extends to spa and hydrotherapy equipment, pond life-support and filtration, and architectural fountain systems — nozzles, pumps and controls for everything from a courtyard feature to a hotel landmark.

Because we engineer these ranges to operate as one system, components are matched for flow, pressure and chemistry rather than bolted together from whatever was in stock. The result is quieter plant rooms, lower energy bills and water that holds its quality through a Gulf summer.

## Design, build and genuine after-sales support

Alongside supply, our design-and-build arm works with consultants, contractors and private clients from concept to commissioning. We size and lay out plant rooms, model circulation and filtration, integrate automation, and hand over systems that are documented and serviceable — not a mystery to whoever maintains them next.

That commitment continues long after handover. Aquora supplies only authentic equipment, sourced through proper channels, so spares, warranties and performance are exactly what they should be. Our team provides ongoing servicing, parts and technical guidance across the UAE and the wider GCC, supporting clients in Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Bahrain and Kuwait.

We build water systems to last — and we stay to look after them.
