# Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Aquora uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, and how you can control them.

## 1. What cookies are
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work, remember your preferences, and — where you allow it — help us understand how the site is used.

## 2. The cookies we use
- **Essential cookies** keep the site working: they remember your cart, keep you signed in, and secure your session. These are always on, as the site cannot function without them.
- **Analytics cookies** (such as Google Analytics, loaded via Google Tag Manager) help us understand which pages and products are popular so we can improve the experience. These are only set **after you consent**.

## 3. Your choices
When you first visit, our consent banner lets you accept or decline non-essential (analytics) cookies. Until you accept, analytics are held in a "denied" state and no analytics cookies are used. You can change your choice at any time by clearing the consent preference in your browser.

## 4. Managing cookies in your browser
You can also control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may stop parts of the site — such as the cart and checkout — from working.

## 5. Third parties
Some cookies are set by trusted third parties that help us run the site, including our analytics and payment providers. Their use of data is governed by their own privacy policies.
