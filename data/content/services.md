# Design, Supply & Installation

Aquora is more than a catalogue. Our engineering team designs, supplies, installs and maintains complete pool, spa and water-feature systems across the UAE and the wider Gulf — so the equipment you buy is specified correctly, installed properly and supported for years.

## Consultation & Hydraulic Design

Every project starts with the water, not the wishlist. We survey the site, confirm pool volume and turnover targets, then size pipework, pumps and filtration to deliver clear, balanced water at the lowest sensible running cost. You receive a clear hydraulic design and an honest equipment schedule before anything is ordered.

## Equipment Specification

We specify from Aquora's own house product lines, matched as a system rather than bolted together from whatever is in stock. Components are chosen for Gulf realities — fine dust, hard water and months of extreme heat — so plant rooms run quieter, cells and media last longer, and energy bills stay sensible.

## Supply

Whether you are a villa owner buying a single pump or a contractor outfitting a development, you get genuine, warranty-backed equipment at transparent AED pricing, held in local stock and dispatched across the Emirates. Bulk and contractor pricing is available on request.

## Installation & Commissioning

Our certified teams install to specification, pressure-test the system and document a full commissioning handover — flow rates, chemical balance, automation settings and a maintenance schedule. You finish with a system that works on day one and a record of exactly how it was set up.

## Fountains & Water Features

We design and build architectural fountains — from still reflecting pools to choreographed light and musical "dancing" fountains for hotels, retail and landmark landscapes. Nozzle selection, pump hydraulics, LED and DMX control and corrosion protection are engineered together so the feature performs reliably in the Gulf climate, not just on opening night.

## Maintenance Contracts

Water systems reward consistent care. Our scheduled maintenance contracts cover everything from a single villa pool to a multi-site hospitality portfolio: routine servicing, water chemistry, descaling, genuine spares and priority call-outs — so small issues never become expensive ones.

## Request a Free Consultation

Tell us about your pool, spa or water feature and our engineers will recommend the right system, sizing and budget — with no obligation. Aquora builds water systems to last, and stays to look after them.
